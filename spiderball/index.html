This is a port of Spiderball4k (see also here) by MÃ¥ns Olson.

Controls:

  • Mouse:
    • Left/right click to shoot orange/red rope in the direction of the pointer
    • Middle click to shoot both ropes
  • Keyboard:
    • Z to shoot orange rope
    • X to shoot red rope
    • C to shoot both ropes
    • R to restart the level
    • Q go to previous level
    • W go to next level
